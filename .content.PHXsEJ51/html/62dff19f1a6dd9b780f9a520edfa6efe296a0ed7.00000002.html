Chronic Conditions
where vaccination becomes all the
more important

Staying healthy is a priority for all of us - and it's especially critical for those with chronic conditions. Vaccination provides the best protection against diseases that could result not only in serious health problems, but missed work, medical bills, and not being able to care for your family.

Asthma
Complications Recommendations
In most of the cases it is observed that adults with asthama are more likely to get complications from the flu. Flu vaccine every year to protect against seasonal flu
Due to asthma, your airways may swell and become blocked with mucus, making it hard to breathe. Tdap vaccine to protect against tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis (whooping cough)
Few vaccine-preventable diseases can also increase swelling of your airways and lungs. Pneumococcal vaccines to protect against serious pneumococcal diseases

Diabetes
Name of the Vaccine Protects Against Disease
Illnesses such as influenza may raise blood glucose level to extremely high levels. Flu vaccine every year to protect against seasonal flu
Higher rates of Hepatitis B have been seen among people with diabetes than the rest of the population. HepB vaccine to protect against Hepatitis B
People with diabetes are prone to serious complications making it harder for the immune system to fight infections. Tdap vaccine to protect against tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis (whooping cough)
People with diabetes are at increased risk for death from lung or blood infection, meningitis. Pneumococcal vaccines to protect against serious pneumococcal diseases

Heart disease
Complications Recommendations
People with heart disease are at high risk for developing serious complications from influenza. Flu vaccine every year to protect against seasonal flu
Heart disease can make it harder for you to fight off certain diseases or make it more likely that you will have serious complications from certain diseases. Tdap vaccine to protect against tetanus, diphtheria, and pertussis (whooping cough)
People with heart disease are also at increased risk for invasive pneumococcal disease, including pneumonia with bloodstream infection. Pneumococcal vaccines to protect against serious pneumococcal diseases

Above compilation is standard recommendation for adult vaccination. It is recommended that you talk to your healthcare professional to check if you need this vaccine.
